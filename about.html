About GEKI

The Growing Economic Knowledge and Innovation Association (GEKI) was founded in February 2024 in Mt Darwin by passionate and forward-thinking youth. It was born out of a realization that there was a need to stimulate economic activity, encourage innovation, and empower local entrepreneurs, especially young people and women.

GEKI is more than an association ‚Äî it is a community engine focused on long-term development. By offering platforms for collaboration, networking, and showcasing talents and businesses, we seek to uplift the Mt Darwin community from within. Our programs aim to attract investment, promote local industries, and build sustainable structures for entrepreneurship and innovation.

Among our major initiatives is the Mt Darwin Business EXPO ‚Äî a dynamic event that celebrates local enterprise, provides temporary job opportunities, and offers exhibitors and sponsors exposure to a growing and eager market. Beyond events, GEKI envisions the development of a permanent Mt Darwin Business Park that will serve as a commercial hub and incubator for future-focused ventures.

With the guidance of our leadership, partners, and the support of the community, GEKI is dedicated to making Mt Darwin a recognized and vibrant center of economic activity in Zimbabwe.
